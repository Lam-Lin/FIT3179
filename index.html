World happiness

The data is from 2008 to 2017 World Happiness from different countries and regions, and compared with national gdp, gip and other variables.

The above map is a comparison of the world-wide per capita GDP and per capita life ladder (happiness). It can be seen that the higher the per capita GDP, the higher the Happiness.

World GDP and GPI Introduction

A total of 157 countries in the world have entered the statistics, and GDP is the x-axis, GPI is the y-axis, and size is the religious percentage. It can be seen that most countries with high GDP have relatively higher regligous percetage and higher GPI rankings.
